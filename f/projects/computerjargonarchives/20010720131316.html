2001-07-19 18:38:10 - Outage is a mixed blessing
It turns out that my power outage has been a mixed blessing. If you haven't heard there's a worm on the loose which is tormenting Microsoft IIS servers across the net. It's duty, install itself into IIS, spread itself to other IIS servers, and wait for July 20th GMT to attack DOS style on whitehouse.gov. At work our servers were hit causing our firewalls to literally melt under the DOS traffic. Word on the street Cisco 675 routers melt under the pressure too. So my Cisco was off all day long. I missed much of the traffic. Then I read this comment on Slashdot about weird requests in Apache logs. Sure enough, I've had a TON of them. So score one for Linux, Apache, and open source. Since I had my router forwarding port 80 to the server that took most of the DOS requests away from the router and sent it to the Linux box. My Linux box has just sat there grinning and barring it.

      2001-07-16 06:34:43 - Eventful weekend

I picked up You are being lied to and Neuromancer at B&N; this weekend. I also happened upon the new Gorillaz disc which is quite good. I would suggest looking for their song Clint Eastwood.       I woke up at roughly 2am to find my power had gone out again. I guess paying nearing 900 bucks in rent still isn't enough to keep the lights on. In my dazed stupor I did manage to reset my clock but I failed to realize that it was Monday morning and I did not set the alarm. Thank god I work better than an atomic clock. The jeweler said if I were in a fire, my ring would come out unscathed. It's tougher and stronger than platinum.
