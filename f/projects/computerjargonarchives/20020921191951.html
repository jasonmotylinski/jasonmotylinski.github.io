Weekend in Review
Posted on 2002-09-16 11:54:46

We didn't do too much this past weekend. Friday night was spent at Heather's brother's wedding. Saturday we made the voyage to Krispy Kreme. I discovered the carmel apple doughnut which quickly became my all-time favorite doughnut. Most of the day Saturday was spent cleaning house or trying to avoid it at all costs. Saturday night we hit Sawatdee in Maple Grove after Rick had tipped me off about it. It was a little different than the few in Minneapolis. For one their spice ranks are WAY off. I ordered by usual chicken Pad Thai at a 3 spicy level. I had to stop eating it after a few bites due to my runny nose and watery eyes. Whew, those white boys in Maple Grove can sure make some spicy thai food. After dinner we saw One Hour Photo which hit the spot. Not too creepy but enough off of mainstream that it kept my interest for the entire film. I would suggest most people see it. Beware though, this recommendation comes from the guy who saw the Blair Witch Project twice in the theaters. I mowed the grass on Sunday and chased the dogs around with the mower. I finished up ALL the laundry. Yes, ALL of it. Even the stuff that's been on the floor since we moved in.

So yes, another eventful weekend spent avoiding painting the house. We are becoming very cleaver at avoiding all fix 'em up projects. Buying dogs, eating doughnuts, all things done to assure we stay too busy to do the hard work. So what am I doing tonight to avoid painting? Well, it appears our bathtub drain has clogged So I'm going to spend a few minutes/hours with a drain augger trying to clear it out.

Apparently Sony doesn't want the new Pearl Jam cd to be bootlegged before it's release in November so they have sent out review copies in a Sony walkman which is glued shut. I wonder if the EULA for the discwill require me to glue shut any player I play the CD in?
