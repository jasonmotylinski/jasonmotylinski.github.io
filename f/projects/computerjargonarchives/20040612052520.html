There is a time when one should not cut hair
Posted on 2004-04-11 20:37:39

Over the last few months I've been in the habit of cutting my hair every Monday morning. After a week's worth of growth I'm typically annoyed by the porcupine style. So prior to leaving for work I've started clipping my hair back down to it's normal and healthy length of an 1/8-inch. Waiting for Monday may also be a sign of my severe procrastination nature as I wait until the last possible minute to shave my head.

I've discovered that clipping my hair in the wee hours of the morning may not be such a good thing. More than once I've gotten to work and realized that I've left a skunk's line up the back or side of my head. I've also managed to knick my ear with the blades.

Now that I have learned my lesson about early morning hair cuts I will start doing it on Sundays....yes, Sundays. So if you are wondering what I'm doing on Sundays, I'm cutting my hair. Right. Yes. I am completely resolved to doing it Sunday.

But you know, I do have other things I should do prior to Monday. Wipe off the bathroom mirror, pet the dogs, fluff the couch pillows. Maybe I'm a little too busy tonight. I'll just wait until tomorrow to do it.
