More Mike Doughty
Posted on 2003-09-30 07:43:26

Mike Doughty is coming back to Minneapolis again this year! Tour dates are on his site. November 15th and he's going to be with a full band. Chances are it's the same band that backs up Dan Wilson. And he's playing First Avenue, which he hasn't done in years. I'm very, very excited. Last show we saw with Mike was half solo and half backed by a band. It was noticable durin his solo set that he was quite excited to just rock out with the band. And rock they did. Watch First Avenue's calendar for ticket information. Last time tickets went fast.
